### About Me

Hey, I'm keta — an indie developer who builds AI agent systems by day and farms virtual crops by night. You'll usually find me vibe coding with music on, crafting multi-agent frameworks while bouncing between playlists.

Beyond code, I'm a lifelong gamer — grew up commanding armies in Warcraft III: The Frozen Throne, got emotionally wrecked by Detroit: Become Human, roamed the wild frontier in Red Dead Redemption 2, caused chaos across Los Santos in GTA V.
